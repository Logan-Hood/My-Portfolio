I’m Logan Hood, a UX/UI designer with a background in B2B sales. For over a decade, I focused on building relationships, solving problems, and helping businesses meet their goals. Along the way, I learned how to listen, ask the right questions, and turn insights into solutions—and that’s exactly what I bring to my work as a designer.

Now, I use those skills to create intuitive, user-centered designs that not only look great but also work seamlessly. I’m comfortable conducting user research, collaborating with stakeholders, and turning ideas into wireframes and prototypes. With a mix of business strategy and design thinking, I’m passionate about building experiences that make sense for both users and businesses.

Outside of design, I train jiu jitsu, which has taught me discipline, adaptability, and how to break down complex problems—skills I carry into my design work. But when I’m not at my desk or on the mats, you’ll probably find me walking through the city or hiking up a trail while petting every dog I come across.
