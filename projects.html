Case Studies

Limitless Baseball

A youth baseball training facility in Utah, needed a modern, user-friendly website to enhance credibility and increase customer engagement. Through strategic user research and a complete UI overhaul, I developed a responsive and visually appealing platform that streamlined navigation and improved accessibility. The redesign led to a significant increase in inquiries and bookings, helping the facility establish stronger relationships with aspiring athletes and their families. This project demonstrates my ability to balance business goals with user needs, ensuring a seamless and engaging digital experience.

WIP App

WIP is an app concept designed to create a collaborative space for designers, students, and hobbyists to share in-progress work, receive feedback, and refine their projects. The goal was to build a community-driven environment where creatives could support each other through structured critiques and mentorship. Through user testing and iteration, I refined the app’s interface to prioritize engagement and ease of use, ensuring a frictionless experience for users looking to showcase their evolving work. This project highlights my expertise in community-driven design, user engagement strategies, and digital product development.

Reef Life Foundation Website Redesign

The Reef Life Foundation is dedicated to restoring marine ecosystems, but their outdated website struggled to effectively communicate their impact and drive donor engagement. My redesign focused on storytelling, transparency, and ease of navigation, creating a visually compelling and informative platform. By refining the donation flow and enhancing content structure, I improved user trust and increased engagement with the foundation’s mission. This project showcases my ability to align digital experiences with nonprofit objectives, ensuring that users are inspired to take action.

Next Step App

Next Step is a mobile app designed to connect individuals seeking guidance with sponsors who can provide support tailored to their specific challenges. Whether users are navigating career changes, personal development, or major life decisions, the platform streamlines the mentorship process through intuitive matching, seamless communication tools, and progress-tracking features. The design prioritizes accessibility and emotional connection, ensuring that users feel supported throughout their journey. This project highlights my ability to craft user-friendly solutions that foster meaningful relationships and drive positive impact.

Get Moving App

Get Moving is a mobile app designed to promote physical activity by turning movement into a rewarding and social experience. With features such as goal tracking, community challenges, and personalized activity recommendations, the app encourages users to stay engaged with their fitness journey. The interface was designed with accessibility and motivation in mind, leveraging behavioral psychology principles to foster long-term habit formation. This project highlights my ability to create user-centric experiences that drive positive behavior change through intuitive design and thoughtful UX strategies.
